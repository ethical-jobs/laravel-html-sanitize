What we do

The St Vincent de Paul Society is a leading provider of community support services and has been assisting people experiencing disadvantage in NSW for more than 130 years. Our people reach out to the most vulnerable in our community through our Conferences, Special Works and Vinnies shops. The Society's mission is to shape a more just and compassionate society by offering a 'hands up' approach to people in need respecting their dignity, sharing our hope and encouraging them to take control of their own destiny.

The Role

The role will provide program policy development and advice on all matters relating to best practice program governance, and further develop and review Support Services' policy frameworks. The role will work in consultation and collaboration with the Senior Management Team and program managers of the Support Services.

The key responsibilities of the role will include:

\n
    \n
  • Leading the development, implementation and maintenance of flexible and cost effective program policies and support frameworks to ensure that these align with the strategic plan and comply with relevant legislative requirements;
    • \n
  • Providing support and advice to Support Services Executive Director and Senior Management team in the planning, development and review of organisational policies.
    • \n
  • Embedding policy into practice and monitoring adherence, including via Quality Working Groups
